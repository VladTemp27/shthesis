Grade 11

- In this grade level, the learner demonstrates communicative ability, reflective/critical thinking, and literary appreciation, develops reading and writing skills as applied to a wide range of materials other than poetry , fiction, and drama, understanding how to solve problems involving rational, exponential, and logarithmic; to solve business related problems, and to apply logic to real-life situations, understanding of matter, motion, electricity, magnetism, light, and the universe from ancient times to the present; applications of physics and chemistry concepts in contexts, appreciation of contemporary art forms, found in the various regions by understanding the elements and principles, understanding of the significant roles of artists from the regions, understanding of the materials and techniques, relevant concepts to plan for a production, and understanding of fitness, sports,exercise in optimizing one’s health as a habit; as requisite for physical activity assessment performance, and as a career opportunity.
