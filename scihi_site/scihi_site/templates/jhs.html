  • Environmental Science & Computer Science.

The unique subject in our curriculum being Environmental Science. The study of the interrelationships between human actions and the environment is the focus of this subject. Environmental Science is an uncommon academic discipline in that it necessitates both scientific knowledge of the natural world and an understanding of how humans interact with it. We look at how human actions affect the environment and how policies, rules, and decisions influence human behavior. We also look at how human behavior, culture, and sociology interact with the environment.

  • Biotechnology & Computer Science.

The unique subject in our curriculum for this level being Biotechnology. This subject discusses how organisms, parts of organisms, or activities carried out by organisms can assist people or make human existence easier. Biotechnology has been used by humans for thousands of years. Agriculture, selective breeding of organisms, and the creation of products such as soy sauce, alcohol, and leavened bread are all instances of biotechnology's lengthy use. While selective breeding of organisms and the use of organisms to generate products are still used in biotechnology, current biotechnology has shifted its attention to exploiting and modifying organisms' DNA.

  • Consumer Chemistry & Research 1.

The unique subject in our curriculum for this level being Consumer Chemistry. This subject is an examination and study of daily chemistry and substances. Organic chemistry, food chemistry, chemistry in everyday life, and chemistry and medicine are among the topics covered. Polymerization and polymer materials require an understanding of organic chemistry. Preservatives, food preservation, food processing, and the chemistry of changes happening during processing, storage, and consumption are among the topics covered in food chemistry. Chemistry in Everyday Life topics are chosen based on their usefulness to pupils as well as their age group's interest. To inspire future innovators, advanced materials are also offered. Chemistry and Medicine is chosen as a chapter for health and wellbeing in order to provide a high quality of life.
