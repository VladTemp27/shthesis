Honorato C. Perez Senior Memorial Science High School is the premiere Science High School in Cabanatuan City. A secondary education curriculum in Cabanatuan City that aims to progressively strive forward to improve quality education it offers through the implementation of various activities, projects, and programs with the help of the faculty, non-teaching personnel, students, and staffs. It is located at Mabini Street Extension, Cabanatuan City, Nueva Ecija.

The school annually conducts a screening process of selecting its students in order to prolong its existence as one of the leading academic institution in the province. The primary plan of its curriculum is to prepare the students for higher studies of various fields of science.

Honorato C. Perez Sr. Memorial Science High School brings out the best in the students through years of enhancing their skills and increasing their knowledge, but it is not limited to that. The teachers of HCPSMSHS always remind that the moral values and the character of the students is just as important as the academic excellence of the students.

History

On April 19, 1985, the late Cabanatuan City Mayor, Honorato C. Perez Sr., founded the Cabanatuan City Science High School with the coordination of the School Division Superintendent of Cabanatuan City, Dr. Ester C. Baylon. Later on, it was renamed into Honorato C. Perez Sr. Memorial Science High School by means of Sangguniang Panlungsod Ordinance No. 96-002, s. 1996 dated February 2, 1996.

During the year 1985, from April 20 to 26, a set of meeting orientations about the operational management and system of the school was held with the parents, teachers, pupils, leaders, and citizens of Cabanatuan, which happened in the following places: Cabanatuan East Central School, Cabanatuan West Central School, Bahay Pulungan, Sangguniang Panlungsod. To inform the other elementary school graduates who might use the opportunity to study in CCSHS, invitation letters and application forms were sent to the local government official in Region III.

On May 20, 1985, the very first entrance examination was given on the qualified applicants. After checking the exam, May 31, 1985, the results were released and posted on City School Division, City School District, and School Offices. And later on, the first 210 students of Cabanatuan City Science High School were able to compete with different levels and to give recognitions to the school.

Until now, students of Honorato C. Perez Senior Memorial Science High School are continuously bringing honor and recognition to the school by showing their excellence and creativity in various competition.

Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:

Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen. Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.
